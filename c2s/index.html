Sequoia C2S Combine

Sequoia C2S Combine is an AWS emulator built to simulate the US Intelligence Community's AWS Region, C2S. C2S is a unique "air-gapped" stand along AWS environment for the US Intelligence Community. C2S and Sequoia Combine are configured with similar restrictions and unique network and authetication specifications.

C2S IC Marketplace Transition

Sequoia C2S Combine enables ISVs a faster, better and safer transition to the C2S IC Marketplace. By emulating the unique constraints that exist in C2S and leverging Sequoia's C2S best practices, the C2S Combine provides assurance that your project has been propertly vetted to avoid unnecessary delays.

C2S Combine Security

Sequoia certified AWS experts have been engaged with C2S from its beginning and understand its unique configuration. Our C2S experts can assist with vulnerability scanning and provide risk assessment, mitigation techniques and design recommendations to meet C2S specific security requirements.
